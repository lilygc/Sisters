Olmos Park, TX  (San Antonio)

A jewel of a shop created for interested and interesting shoppers. A beautifully curated selection of vintage and new clothes and accessories — folk art, Texana, MCM art glass, silver from Mexico, and more.

Step inside and you'll understand immediately. Sisters in the Park has the feeling of a Parisian salon from another era — rooms filled with beautiful things you simply can't take your eyes off, each one chosen because it earns its place. A vintage dress that fits as though it was made for you. A piece of Mexican silver that catches the light just so. A painting that finds you before you find it. Nothing here is accidental. Everything is worth perusing, learning more about, and anticipating bringing home.

Sisters in the Park is the rare shop where the perfect gift practically finds itself. Browse for someone you love — or let them choose for themselves with a gift certificate, available in the shop.
